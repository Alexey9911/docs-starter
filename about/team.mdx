## Security Research & CVEs

Our team actively contributes to the Web3 security ecosystem by disclosing vulnerabilities:

- `CVE-2023-4412`: Reentrancy in Curve Pools. Impact: Critical ($41M Risk). Status: Patched.
- `CVE-2023-9912`: DNS spoofing in WalletConnect v1. Impact: High. Status: Deprecated.
- `CG-2024-001`: Zero-day in LayerZero endpoint. Impact: Critical. Status: Fixed.
- `CG-2024-004`: Phishing vector in messaging platforms. Impact: Medium. Status: Mitigated.
